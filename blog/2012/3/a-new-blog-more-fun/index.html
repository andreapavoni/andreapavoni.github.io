A new blog, more fun

A bit of history

Until today, I’ve used tumblr for blogging, however I wasn’t too much happy with it, I don’t use all that features (photos, links, and so on), instead I need some more power when writing tech articles. That’s also why I didn’t write too much: every time I got some useful thing to say, I didn’t want to go on tumblr.

After some thought, I decided to write my own blog engine, it was a decent excuse to play with cool toys. Then, a month ago I purchased a VPS on Linode and started playing with (well known) tools and solutions that I’ve never used, such as Arch linux, unicorn and monit.

I’ve also imported some article from my previous blog, as you can see, they were very rare, expect some more writings in the near future ;-)
